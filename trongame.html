TRON GAME

This game is a tribute to the the lightcycle race of the original tron game realeased in 1982.

Game Rules

This game is fairly simple!
In the movie, the competitors were forced into a life or death game where the Light Cycle bikes would create lines wherever they went. If a bike crashed into a line, the rider would be disqualified.
And here is the same! Make your opponent crash into your line!
